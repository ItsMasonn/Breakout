How to Play Breakout!

Don't worry! Playing Breakout is easy, and I'll teach you how to play!! Just follow my lead

     
  1. First, navigate to the main Breakout game
  2. Use the left () and right () arrow keys to move the blue, paddle-like object. If you are confused, it appears as a small platform.
  3. Using the arrow keys to move the paddle, let the ball touch the brick-like structures, then back to hit your paddle. Note: Do NOT let the ball touch the ground.
  4. Each time the ball hits one of the brick-like structures, you will earn a point. You can see the number of points you received in the top left, titled "Score".
  5. If the ball touches the ground once, you will lose a life. You will only have three lives, so use them wisely!
  6. Upon using up all 3 lives, the game will prompt you that your game is over. Additionally, the game will restart.
